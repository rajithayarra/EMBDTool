🔬 Technology Behind MindCare

Our system uses sophisticated logistic regression models trained on extensive datasets of student mental health patterns. The algorithms analyze multiple lifestyle factors including:

  • 📱 Screen time and digital device usage
  • 👥 Social media engagement patterns
  • 📚 Study habits and academic workload
  • 😴 Sleep patterns and quality
  • 🎭 Entertainment and leisure activities
